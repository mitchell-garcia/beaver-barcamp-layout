Welcome to Beaver Barcamp 11

What Is Beaver Barcamp?

BarCamp is an ad-hoc gathering born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment. It is an intense event with discussions, demos, and interaction from attendees.

What kind of sessions happen at Beaver BarCamp? All kinds! While most may be tech oriented, we encourage any kind of PG-13 session. You can see a list of Beaver BarCamp Session Ideas to get an idea of what you may find.

We will be posting updates on our Twitter account @beaverbarcamp. You can also connect on live chat via IRC - irc.freenode.net, #BeaverBarCamp ( How To Set Up IRC ). There is also a Beaver Barcamp Google Group to get important notifications on the event.
